Artist Info

Borislav "Bobby" Slavov is an award-winning video game Composer and Music Director who began his career in 2001. He created his first soundtrack for the real-time strategy video game, Knights of Honor. He is also known as Composer and Music Director for the games Crysis 2, Crysis 3, Ryse: Son of Rome, Two Worlds II, and the upcoming Baldur's Gate 3.
